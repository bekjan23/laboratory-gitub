OUR STORY

Founded in 1987, Atech®, headquartered in Taipei, Taiwan, is a recognized leader in private label computer peripherals with established, efficient distribution channels for the global market. Thanks to an advanced development team and its own, independent expertise, Atech® continues to produce a high-tech product that is required for the most discerning consumer. The philosophy of the company is based on the principle - to embody new ideas with the help of technology.
